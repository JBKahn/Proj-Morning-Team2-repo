Build a Schedule worth following

If you want to get organized and on top of your classes than you have come to the right place. AbArticulus helps you discover and report due dates, class and social events happening in or around campus.

If you've ever missed a test, a deadline or simply misprioritized your work than we can help. Using sophisticated measurements and algorithms, we'll suggest a study schedule for you and help you build it.

Best of all, it's completely built on Google Calendar and so you'll be able to view and edit events from your phone as well as share your calendar with others. Get started today!
